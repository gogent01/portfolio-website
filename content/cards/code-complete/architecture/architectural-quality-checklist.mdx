  What are the criteria to consider when assessing the quality of a project architecture? (Name 8)
publishedOn: '2024-11-11T12:00:00+0100'
id: 0uCPTY
---
- does the architecture cover all requirements?
- are the goals of the architecture clear?
- is the architecture natural, clear, and solid?
- is any part over-architected or under-architected?
- is the architecture (mostly) machine- and language-independent?
- are motivations for all major decisions provided?
- are risk areas and risk minimization strategies specified?
- are programmers comfortable with the architecture?
